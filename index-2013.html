Skills

General
I work quickly and efficiently on deadline, with sharp attention to detail. Adaptable to new workflows, CMSes and environments. Experienced with computer-assisted reporting methods, with some training in geographic information systems (GIS). Some experience/training in agile/scrum methodology.
Graphic Design
I work most in Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator — though since I largely design in code these days, I use these applications less often than before. For map-making, I typically use either ArcMap (exported to Illustrator for clean-up) or TileMill/MapBox. I previously have worked with Adobe Flash (extensively), Adobe InDesign, Quark XPress and Adobe Acrobat.
Web Development
My preferred workflow involves frequent cmd/alt-tabbing between a basic text editor (TextWrangler) and Google Chrome. I work a lot with HTML and CSS (in their various flavors), jQuery, Twitter Bootstrap (for responsive designs) and Github. I've done limited explorations with Raphael and D3 for data visualization (though you might be surprised how far you can get just with proportionally-sized/placed divs — no special libraries required). In the past, I have done some work with PHP, ColdFusion and MySQL.
